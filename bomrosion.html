Worcoast – a custom realistic forest and mountain terrain

Worcoast is a huge map. Its 4000×4000 and takes ca 15 minutes to cross, with over 100 wonderful places, to build, explore and adventure. This map by McMeddon is the latest Map after the Map “Distordes”. This map includes huge Mountain ranges across the map, huge snow and glaciers falls and big warm plans + tundra places to visit and build. It contains many small riffs and caverns between the mountains and makes an optimal place to live. Also, to find are the caverns, caves and oceans surrounding the map.

Kenmaya – A Full Winter Glacier Terrain

Kenmaya is a small 2k Winter Glacier Survival Friendly Terrain for Java and Bedrock made by McMeddon. It contains galciers, pathways, iceflows and huge snowy forest alongside huge snow dunes.

Distordes – A realistic 4K Winter Terrain

Distordes is a medium-sized 4k Winter Glacier Survival Friendly Terrain for Java and Bedrock made by McMeddon. It contains glaciers, pathways, ice flows and huge snowy tundra and classical forests to get lost in. Explore the wild frozen ice of the north and skinny islands on the sea. Enjoy the wide grass fields of the south and travel alongside the coast on rocky beaches.

This map was a follow-up map to kenmaya, a full winter map. The key idea behind this map was the frozen sea, the extremely large and Rocky Mountains as far as your eyes can travel you. Unfortunately, the height differences are not overwhelming in the mountains due to the scale of those. The coast is beautiful and you find every few meters a wonderful new spot.
