Full Description

Kantatur

Kantatur is a realistic island… it’s 3 km by 3 km made by McMeddon. It’s an Island that is “originally shaped like the number eight” But hardly eroded and transformed in the creation process. A custom realistic world with rare amount of trees and little forests, its a rough snowy world with rocks and minimal vegetation. Due to its location it’s nearly completely covered in snow, its massive mountains have gathered a lot of and let it flow all the down, covering half of the island in its porcess. Rivers have emerged that lead you all the way from coast to beak. It is a fun way to explore the world. Settle along the little rivers that come down the snowy mountains and set up a fire.
